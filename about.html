About

A studio for quiet intensity and bold imagination.

Studio Nono is an independent creative studio shaped by cinematic craft, musical sensitivity and a belief in deeply human stories.

Our point of view

We create work that feels refined, intimate and transportive. Every project begins with a clear emotional question, then finds its form through image, performance, sound and rhythm.

Our collaborators include filmmakers, composers, producers, designers and cultural partners who value patience, precision and memorable atmosphere.
